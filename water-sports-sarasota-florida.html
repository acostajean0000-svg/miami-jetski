❓ FAQs about Water Sports in Sarasota

What water sports can I do in Sarasota?▼
Sarasota offers jet ski rentals, kayaking, paddleboarding, fishing charters, sunset boat cruises, and parasailing from Sarasota Bay and the Gulf of Mexico.
Is Siesta Key good for water sports?▼
Yes — Siesta Key is one of the best beaches in the US for water sports. The calm, turquoise waters are perfect for kayaking, paddleboarding, and jet skiing.
How far is Sarasota from Tampa for a day trip water sports?▼
Sarasota is about 60 miles south of Tampa (about 1 hour drive), making it an easy day trip. Many visitors combine Sarasota with a beach day at Siesta Key.
