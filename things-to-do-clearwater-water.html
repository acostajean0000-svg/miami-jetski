🌊 Clearwater Beach, FL — Complete Water Guide

Things to Do Clearwater Beach — Water Sports 2025

The definitive guide to on-water activities at America's #1 beach — from dolphin tours and Caladesi Island trips to deep sea fishing and Gulf sunset cruises.

10 Best Water Activities at Clearwater Beach

01

Dolphin Watching Tour

Cruise Clearwater Harbor where 100+ resident dolphins live year-round. Sightings are essentially guaranteed.

From $35 pp · 1.5–2 hrs
02

Boat Rental to Caladesi Island

Rent a boat and cross to one of Florida's finest undeveloped barrier islands. 3 miles of pristine Gulf beach.

$250–$450 · Half day · Chadillac
03

Gulf Sunset Cruise

Watch the famous Gulf Coast sunset from a boat west of Clearwater Beach. Dolphins often join at dusk.

From $40 pp · 2 hrs
04

Deep Sea Fishing — Hubbard's Marina

Half-day or full-day grouper and snapper trips on the Gulf party boats from nearby Madeira Beach.

From $55 pp · Hubbard's
05

Parasailing

Soar 400–800 feet above the Gulf of Mexico for panoramic views of the barrier island chain and Tampa Bay.

$65–$95 pp · Per flight
06

Kayak / Paddleboard

Paddle the protected cove and mangrove channels of Clearwater Harbor at your own pace.

$20–$40 pp/hr · Chadillac Cove
07

Shell Key Shelling Excursion

Boat trip to uninhabited Shell Key island — excellent for lightning whelks, conchs, and sand dollars.

$35–$55 pp · Hubbard's Marina
08

Jet Ski Rental

Ride the Gulf or harbor waters on a jet ski. Clearwater Beach has several rental operators near Pier 60.

$90–$130 pp/hr
09

Tampa Bay Helicopter Tour

See the full scale of Tampa Bay, the Sunshine Skyway Bridge, and the barrier island chain from the air.

From $55 pp · Book tour
10

Tampa Riverwalk Kayaking

30 minutes across the bay: paddle downtown Tampa's Hillsborough River with skyline views. Great contrast to the beach.

From $25 pp/hr · Riverwalk Rentals

Sample 2-Day Clearwater Beach Water Itinerary

🗓️ Best of Clearwater Beach — 2 Days on the Water

Day 1 — Harbor & Islands

8:30 a.m. — Pick up boat rental from Chadillac Adventures. Head north through Clearwater Harbor (dolphin sightings almost certain). 10:00 a.m. — Arrive Caladesi Island, anchor and walk the pristine Gulf beach. Lunch on the boat or the island's small concession. 1:00 p.m. — Return south, explore the harbor grass flats and mangrove channels. Return boat by 3:00 p.m. 7:00 p.m. — Sunset cruise from Clearwater Beach marina — Gulf of Mexico sunset with dolphins.

Day 2 — Fishing, Kayaking & Madeira Beach

7:00 a.m. — Half-day deep sea fishing party boat departure from Hubbard's Marina, Madeira Beach. Target grouper and snapper on the nearshore ledges. 12:30 p.m. — Return to Johns Pass Village, lunch at waterfront restaurant. Walk the boardwalk. 3:00 p.m. — Drive north to Clearwater, kayak or paddleboard the Cove Cay waterway from Chadillac Adventures. 6:30 p.m. — Sunset at Pier 60, Clearwater Beach — the nightly festival with street performers and perfect Gulf views.
