Key Largo calls itself the "Diving Capital of the World" — and with good reason. As the northernmost island in the Florida Keys chain, it's the closest point in the continental US to a living coral reef, and the gateway to John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park, the first underwater park in the US. Key Largo is just 45 minutes from Miami, making it the most accessible water sports destination in the Florida Keys for day-trippers and weekenders from South Florida.

Beyond snorkeling and diving, Key Largo offers jet skiing, kayaking, stand-up paddleboarding, boat rentals, and fishing charters — all in crystal-clear tropical water.

📍 Best Spots for Water Activities in Key Largo

John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park

The anchor of Key Largo water sports. The park's concession offers snorkel tours, glass-bottom boat tours, kayak rentals, and scuba diving all in one location. The park's waters cover 70 square miles and protect a rich section of the reef tract. Arrive early — it fills up fast on weekends.

Buttonwood Sound

The protected bay between Key Largo and the mainland. Calm, shallow, and perfect for jet skiing, kayaking, and paddleboarding. Several operators launch from marina ramps directly onto the sound, giving you immediate access to clear, flat water.

Molasses Reef

The crown jewel of Key Largo snorkeling. Located 6 miles offshore, Molasses is a large, diverse reef rising to within 6 feet of the surface. Expect extraordinary fish life, brain corals, sea fans, and almost guaranteed sea turtle sightings. Tour boats depart from the park and from several independent operators.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Key Largo a good day trip from Miami?

One of the best. It's 45–55 minutes from Miami, there's plenty of parking, and you can snorkel, jet ski, or kayak for a full day and be back before dark. Midweek is much less crowded than weekends.

What is the best water activity in Key Largo?

For most visitors, reef snorkeling is the standout experience — it's unlike anything you can do anywhere else in the continental US. Jet skiing on Buttonwood Sound is the most fun for pure adrenaline. Kayaking through the mangroves is the most peaceful.

How is the water clarity in Key Largo?

Excellent by mainland Florida standards. The bay side is clear with sandy bottom visible in 8–12 feet. The Atlantic side at the reef has visibility of 30–80 feet depending on conditions. Best after a stretch of calm weather.
