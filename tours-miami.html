🏆 Must-Do Tours in Miami

Wynwood Graffiti & Art Tour

Wynwood Walls is one of the world's largest outdoor art installations. Take a guided walking or golf cart tour to get the inside story on the murals, artists, and the neighborhood's transformation. Best on weekday mornings.

Biscayne Bay Boat Tour

Cruise past the mansions of Star Island, anchor at the famous Miami Sandbar, and see the Miami skyline from the water. A 2–3 hour private or group boat tour is one of the best ways to experience Miami.

Everglades Airboat Adventure

Glide through the "River of Grass" on a flat-bottomed airboat. Spot alligators, herons, and exotic Florida wildlife. Most Everglades operators are 25–45 minutes from downtown Miami.

Key West Day Trip

Several operators run full-day bus or ferry trips from Miami to Key West. You'll explore the southernmost city in the continental US, see Ernest Hemingway's house, and enjoy the island's laid-back vibe — all in one day.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most popular tour in Miami?

The Everglades airboat tour and Biscayne Bay boat tour are consistently among the most booked. The Wynwood Art Walk is the most popular walking experience.

How far is the Everglades from Miami?

The main Everglades entrance is about 25–40 minutes southwest of downtown Miami. Most tour operators provide transportation from Miami hotels.
