❓ FAQs about Water Sports in Fort Lauderdale

What water sports are available in Fort Lauderdale?▼
Fort Lauderdale offers jet ski rentals, fishing charters, boat tours, kayaking, paddleboarding, and snorkeling from the Intracoastal Waterway, Port Everglades, and Hollywood Beach.
How much does a boat rental cost in Fort Lauderdale?▼
Boat rentals in Fort Lauderdale start from around $300–$600 for a half day. Fishing charters run $500–$1,500. Jet ski rentals start around $99–$149 for 30 minutes.
Do I need a license to rent a jet ski in Fort Lauderdale?▼
Anyone born after January 1, 1988 must carry a Florida Boating Safety ID. Most operators provide a free safety orientation on-site.
