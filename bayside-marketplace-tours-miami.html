💡 Tips for Visiting Bayside Marketplace

🕐

Arrive Early

Weekends and school holidays sell out fast. Booking online the night before guarantees your spot and sometimes earns a small discount.

🅿️

Skip the Parking Battle

Take the free Metromover to Bayfront Park station — it's a 2-minute walk to Bayside and avoids the $15+ garage fee on busy days.

🌦️

Watch the Weather

Miami's afternoon thunderstorms (June–September) can delay or cancel outdoor tours. Book morning slots for the most reliable departures.

🍽️

Eat Before You Cruise

Bayside has dozens of restaurants. Grab a bite before your tour — most boat experiences don't include food and bar prices onboard are premium.

☀️

Sunscreen Is Essential

Open water + Florida sun = serious burn in 20 minutes. Bring SPF 50+ and a hat. Most vendors don't sell sunscreen at the dock.

💳

Book Online for Best Rate

Walk-up ticket prices at Bayside can be 15–25% higher than online rates. All 13 operators on this page have instant online booking.

📍 Bayside Marketplace Location

All tours depart from or very near 401 Biscayne Blvd, Miami, FL 33132 — on the downtown waterfront, directly on Biscayne Bay.

🚇

Getting There: Metromover (free) stops at Bayfront Park station — a 2-min walk. Brightline's MiamiCentral station is 10 minutes on foot. Rideshare drop-off is at the Biscayne Blvd entrance. If driving, park in the Bayside garage at 400 Biscayne Blvd (hourly rates apply).
