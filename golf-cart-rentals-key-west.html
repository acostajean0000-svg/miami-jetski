❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do you need a driver's license to rent a golf cart in Key West?▼
Yes. All Key West golf cart rentals require a valid driver's license. Drivers must be at least 21 years old (some operators allow 18+). Golf carts are street-legal in Key West on roads with speed limits up to 35 mph.
How much does it cost to rent a golf cart in Key West?▼
Golf cart rentals in Key West start from $89/day. Premium or larger carts range from $199–$300. Multi-day rentals often include a 10–20% discount.
Are golf carts street-legal in Key West?▼
Yes! Key West is one of the few US cities where golf carts are fully street-legal on public roads with speed limits up to 35 mph. Drive anywhere from Duval Street to the Southernmost Point.
Where can I pick up my golf cart rental in Key West?▼
Pickup locations are along N Roosevelt Blvd and central Key West. All three operators are shown on the map on this page with exact addresses.
Can I park a golf cart anywhere in Key West?▼
Golf carts use regular parking spaces — not bike lanes or sidewalks. Paid lots on Duval and Simonton streets accommodate golf carts. Many restaurants and shops offer golf cart-friendly parking.
When should I book a golf cart rental in Key West?▼
Carts sell out quickly during Fantasy Fest (October), Spring Break (March), and holiday weekends. Book at least 2–3 days ahead year-round. Weekday availability outside peak season is often same-day.
