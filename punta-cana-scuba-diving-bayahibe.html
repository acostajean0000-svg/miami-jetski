FAQ

Do I need scuba certification? +
Beginner intro dives are available with no certification required. Certified divers can book advanced and specialty dives.
What dive sites are available? +
Catalina Island reefs, St. George wreck, and several coral wall dives near Bayahíbe.
How many people per charter? +
Private charters for 2–8 divers. Ideal for groups, couples, and families.
What is the water visibility? +
Visibility typically 20–30 meters in Bayahíbe waters. Best conditions October–May.
Can I book same day? +
Subject to availability. Advance booking recommended for private charters.
