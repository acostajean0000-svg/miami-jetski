Jet skiing in the Florida Keys is an experience in a class of its own. The water here is cleaner, clearer, and more turquoise than almost anywhere else in the continental US. You can see the reef 20 feet below you as you skim across the surface. On a calm morning in Key Largo or Marathon, it feels more like the Caribbean than mainland Florida — because geologically and ecologically, it is.

The Keys offer jet skiing along protected bay waters, in the open Atlantic near the reef line, and through scenic channels between islands. Each area has its own character and appeal.

📍 Best Jet Ski Spots in the Florida Keys

Key Largo — Buttonwood Sound & Card Sound

The northernmost key and the most accessible from Miami (just 45 minutes south). Buttonwood Sound on the bay side offers calm, protected water with stunning mangrove scenery. Card Sound to the north is wide open and great for speed runs.

Grassy Key / Marathon

Centrally located in the island chain. Ride out toward the famous Seven Mile Bridge or explore the flats of the Florida Bay. Grassy Key Marina is one of the best launching points in the middle Keys.

Key West Harbor

The most scenic and action-packed jet ski setting in the Keys. Cruise past historic Key West waterfront, the commercial fishing fleet, and the liveaboard boats in the harbor. Six Fins runs guided 2-hour tours from here that are consistently rated among the best in the Keys.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I ride a jet ski near the reef in the Florida Keys?

You can ride alongside the reef — and the views through the crystal-clear water are spectacular. Riding directly over coral is prohibited and enforced. All licensed operators mark reef protection zones.

Is the water safe to swim in the Florida Keys?

Yes. The Florida Keys have some of the cleanest coastal water in the continental US. Water temperatures average 75–85°F year-round. No dangerous currents in the bay; mild currents on the Atlantic side near channels.

How far in advance should I book jet ski rentals in the Keys?

1 week minimum for weekdays; 2–3 weeks for Key West weekends. Spring break and holiday weekends sell out 4–6 weeks ahead.
