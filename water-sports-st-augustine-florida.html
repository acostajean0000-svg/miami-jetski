❓ FAQs about Water Sports in St. Augustine

What water sports are available in St. Augustine?▼
St. Augustine offers kayak and paddleboard rentals, dolphin and eco tours, fishing charters, boat rentals on the Matanzas River and Intracoastal Waterway, and jet skiing near the beaches.
Can I kayak in St. Augustine?▼
Absolutely — kayaking is one of the most popular activities. Rentals are available near the Vilano Beach area and Old Town. Guided eco-tours are popular for seeing dolphins, manatees, and shorebirds.
What is the best water activity in St. Augustine for families?▼
Dolphin watching boat tours are the most popular family activity. Most tours run 90 minutes and are suitable for all ages. Some operators also offer night cruises.
