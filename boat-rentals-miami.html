❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a boat in Miami?▼
Boat options in Miami start from $5 for group tours. Self-drive rentals start at $260. Mid-size boats rent from $349–$795. Yachts and mega yachts range from $900–$3,800+ depending on size and duration.
Do you need a license to rent a boat in Miami?▼
Florida requires a Boater Safety ID card for anyone born after January 1, 1988. Many operators offer captained charters so no license is needed.
Where can I rent a boat in Miami?▼
Main pickup locations: Miami River, Downtown Bayside, Miami Beach Marina, and Key Biscayne. See the interactive map on this page.
Can I rent a boat without a captain?▼
Yes — several operators offer self-drive (bare boat) rentals for qualified boaters. Captained charters are available from most operators if you prefer.
What's the best boat experience in Miami?▼
For value: Bruschi Small Boat from $260. Most booked: 100 Miami Bayshore (5.0★, 377 reviews). Best tour: Miami Aqua Tours from $5. Luxury: 100 Miami NW 21st Mega Yacht.
