❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a boat in Miami?▼
Boat rentals in Miami start from $260 for a small motorboat. Mid-size boats rent from $349–$795. Yachts and mega yachts range from $900–$3,800+ depending on size and duration.
Do you need a license to rent a boat in Miami?▼
Florida requires a Boater Safety ID card for anyone born after January 1, 1988. Some rental operators offer captained charters so no license is needed. Always check with the operator before booking.
Where can I rent a boat in Miami?▼
Main pickup locations include Miami River, Bayside/Downtown, Miami Beach Marina, and Key Biscayne. All operator locations are shown on the interactive map on this page.
Can I rent a boat without a captain in Miami?▼
Yes. Several Miami operators offer self-drive (bare boat) rentals for qualified boaters. If you don't have experience, captained charters are available from most operators on this page.
What's the best area to rent a boat in Miami?▼
Miami River is popular for budget rentals. Biscayne Bay offers the iconic Miami skyline backdrop. Miami Beach Marina gives quick ocean access. Key Biscayne is ideal for calm, crystal-clear water and sandbar visits.
What can I do on a boat rental in Miami?▼
Popular activities include visiting Biscayne Bay sandbars, exploring Miami's waterfront skyline, snorkeling at the reef, anchoring near Star Island, sunset cruises, and island hopping to Key Biscayne or Bayside.
