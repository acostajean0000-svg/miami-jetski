❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What activities can you do in Palm Beach?▼
Palm Beach offers snorkeling tours, boat charters, kayaking through mangroves, paddleboarding, ghost tours, and shark dive experiences.
How much do Palm Beach activities cost?▼
Snorkeling tours from $69. Kayaking from $35. Paddleboarding from $45. Boat charters from $250. Premium experiences up to $1,000+.
What is the best activity in Palm Beach?▼
Snorkeling at the natural reef is top-rated. Kayaking through Jupiter Inlet is most scenic. Night shark snorkeling is most adventurous.
Are there good snorkeling spots near Palm Beach?▼
Yes — the Palm Beach inlet area has one of the closest natural reefs to shore in Florida. Several operators offer guided snorkel tours.
