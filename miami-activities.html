❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What activities can you do in Miami?▼
Miami offers jet ski rentals, private boat charters, yacht experiences, parasailing, ATV tours, waterfront golf cart tours, flyboard, and deep-sea fishing. All bookable instantly via FareHarbor.
How much do Miami water activities cost?▼
Jet ski rentals start from $79/hr. Party boat charters from $169. Private yacht charters from $400. Parasailing from $100. ATV adventures from $75. Premium experiences up to $3,000+.
What is the best water activity in Miami?▼
Jet ski rentals on Biscayne Bay are the top activity for thrills. Private boat charters for groups. Sunset yacht cruises for romance. Flyboard sessions for the adventurous.
Do I need a license for Miami water sports?▼
No license for guided tours or jet ski rentals with an operator. A Florida Boater Safety ID is required if you self-captain a vessel and were born after January 1, 1988.
Can I book Miami activities same day?▼
Yes. Most Miami operators offer same-day availability via FareHarbor. Instant confirmation on most bookings.
