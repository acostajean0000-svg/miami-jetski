Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your jet ski adventure.

In Florida, anyone born on or after January 1, 1988 must complete a boating safety course to operate a personal watercraft. Don't worry — all our operators include a certified instructor who will brief you on safety before you ride. No prior experience needed!
Yes! All 21 operators listed on our site include a certified instructor in the price shown. The instructor accompanies you on the water, guides you through the experience, and ensures your safety at all times.
The most affordable option on our platform is the Jetski Rentals Marathon (Seahorse Adventures) starting at just $99/session. For Miami area, the cheapest options are in Hollywood ($110) and South Beach ($125). Pompano Beach also offers $100 starting prices.
We're a comparison platform. When you click "Book Now" on any operator card, you're taken directly to their secure booking page on FareHarbor — a trusted booking platform used by thousands of activity companies. You pay directly to the operator, not to us.
We recommend bringing: swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, sunglasses with a strap, and a valid photo ID. Leave valuables at home or in your car. Operators typically provide life jackets. Water shoes are optional but recommended.
Most jet skis are designed for 2-3 passengers. Yes, you can bring a passenger — it's a great couples or friends activity! Some operators may charge a small additional fee for extra passengers. Check the specific operator's booking page for details.
