❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What activities can you do on the Gulf Coast?▼
The Gulf Coast offers jet ski rentals, jet car (amphibious vehicle) rides, boat charters, kayaking through mangroves, parasailing, and deep-sea fishing near Naples, Fort Myers, and Marco Island.
How much do Gulf Coast activities cost?▼
Jet ski rentals from $99/hr. Jet car rides from $79. Kayak tours from $35. Boat charters from $200. Parasailing from $89. Deep-sea fishing from $300.
What is the best Gulf Coast water activity?▼
Jet car rides are unique to the Gulf Coast and top-rated for novelty. Jet ski rentals on Naples Bay are most popular. Kayaking through mangroves for nature lovers.
Is the Gulf Coast warm enough for water sports?▼
Yes — the Gulf of Mexico is typically 2-5°F warmer than the Atlantic side. Water sports are comfortable year-round, with peak season November–April.
Can I book Gulf Coast activities same day?▼
Yes. Most operators offer same-day availability via FareHarbor with instant confirmation.
