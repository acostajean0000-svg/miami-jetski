📍 Best Launch Spots in Miami

Biscayne Bay

The most popular area for jet skis in Miami. The bay is protected from ocean swells, making it ideal for beginners and families. You'll get views of the Downtown Miami skyline and can cruise past Star Island and Watson Island. Most operators near the Coconut Grove Marina launch from here.

South Beach / Miami Beach Marina

For those who want open water and more speed, the Atlantic side near South Beach delivers. Expect choppier conditions but a more exhilarating ride. Operators along Collins Avenue or near the marina offer direct ocean access.

North Miami Beach

A quieter alternative with calmer waters. Great if you want to avoid the South Beach crowds while still having a full Miami experience. Several operators run out of Haulover Park and Aventura marina.

📋 What You Need to Know Before Renting

  • Age requirement: Riders must be at least 16 years old. Under 18 requires a parent or guardian signature.
  • Florida law: Anyone born after January 1, 1988 must complete a boating safety course. Most operators provide an on-site orientation that satisfies this.
  • What's included: Life jackets, orientation, and fuel are typically included in the rental price.
  • Deposit: Most operators require a credit card hold of $200–$500 for damages.
  • Weather policy: Rentals are typically rescheduled at no cost if cancelled due to bad weather.

🤿 Combo Experiences Worth Considering

Several Miami operators bundle jet ski rentals with other activities for a better value. Popular combos include jet ski + parasailing, jet ski + party boat, and jet ski + snorkeling tour. If you're spending a full day on the water, a combo package can save you 20–30% versus booking separately.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does jet ski rental cost in Miami?

Expect to pay between $65 and $120 per hour. A 30-minute session starts around $40. Prices vary by operator, jet ski model (single vs. two-person), and season.

Do I need a license to rent a jet ski in Miami?

Florida requires a boating safety course for anyone born after January 1, 1988. Most rental operators offer a quick on-site orientation that satisfies this requirement — no advance certification needed.

Can two people ride one jet ski in Miami?

Yes. Most operators offer two-person (tandem) jet skis that can accommodate a rider and a passenger. The price is typically the same as a single-rider unit.

What is the best time of year to rent a jet ski in Miami?

Miami's weather is great year-round, but November through April is the sweet spot — comfortable temperatures, lower humidity, and calm water conditions. Avoid hurricane season (August–October) when conditions can be unpredictable.
