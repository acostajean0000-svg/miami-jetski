FAQ – Canelo Tours

What activities does Canelo Tours offer? +
ATV tours, snorkeling, boat rentals, zip-line, and custom combo packages.
Is driving experience needed for ATVs? +
No, basic orientation provided. All ATVs are automatic and easy to operate.
What is the minimum age for ATV tours? +
Riders must be 16+. Children can ride as passengers with an adult driver.
Do they offer hotel pickup? +
Yes, hotel pickup and drop-off available from major Punta Cana resorts.
How long are the tours? +
ATV tours 2-3 hours. Snorkeling 2 hours. Combo packages 4-6 hours.
