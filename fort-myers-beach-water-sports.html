Fort Myers Beach Water Sports 2025

Jet skis, tiki boat cruises, pontoon rentals, and dolphin tours on the Gulf — Fort Myers Beach is Southwest Florida's most active water sports hub.

Water Sports on the Gulf at Fort Myers Beach

Fort Myers Beach occupies a 7-mile barrier island between the Gulf of Mexico and Estero Bay, giving water sports operators access to two very different bodies of water. The Gulf side offers open ocean, white sand, and warm clear water for jet skiing, parasailing, and swimming. The Estero Bay side — a protected estuary and part of the Great Calusa Blueway paddling trail — is calm, shallow, and perfect for kayaking, paddleboarding, and pontoon cruising.

The beach was significantly impacted by Hurricane Ian in September 2022, but by 2025 many businesses have rebuilt and returned to operation. The water sports scene is rebounding strongly, and Fort Myers Beach remains one of the best destinations on Florida's Gulf Coast for a full day of water-based activities.

The signature Fort Myers Beach water experience is the Tiki Boat — a pontoon styled like a floating tiki bar, where groups of up to 14 cruise Estero Bay with their own drinks, music, and a friendly captain. This BYOB concept has become wildly popular across Florida, and Big Tiki Tours' Fort Myers Beach operation is one of the most fun and original in the state.

Fort Myers Beach Water Sports FAQ

What water sports are available at Fort Myers Beach?

Fort Myers Beach offers jet ski rentals on the Gulf and Estero Bay, pontoon boat rentals, Tiki boat cruises, dolphin watching tours, parasailing, kayaking, paddleboarding, and fishing charters targeting snook, redfish, and offshore species.

What is the Tiki boat at Fort Myers Beach?

The Tiki boat is a pontoon styled like a floating tiki bar, offering BYOB cruises through Estero Bay. Big Tiki Tours is the main operator. These cruises are hugely popular for bachelorette parties, birthdays, and group celebrations.

Is Fort Myers Beach good for families?

Yes — Fort Myers Beach is very family-friendly. The calm Estero Bay side is perfect for beginner kayaking, paddleboarding, and pontoon rentals. Dolphin watching tours are popular with kids, and banana boat rides are available for groups.

How much do jet ski rentals cost at Fort Myers Beach?

Jet ski rentals at Fort Myers Beach typically run $90–$130 for 30 minutes or $160–$220 for one hour. Sunset Ski & Watersports and Sunsplash are among the top operators.

Is Fort Myers Beach open after Hurricane Ian?

Fort Myers Beach was severely impacted by Hurricane Ian in 2022 and has been rebuilding since. By 2025, the beach and many water sports operators have returned to operation. Always confirm directly with operators before booking.
