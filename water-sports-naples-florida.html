❓ FAQs about Water Sports in Naples

What water activities are in Naples, FL?▼
Naples offers kayak and paddleboard eco-tours through the Ten Thousand Islands, fishing charters, boat rentals, dolphin tours, and jet ski rentals along the Gulf of Mexico.
When is the best time to visit Naples for water sports?▼
November through April is peak season with calm Gulf waters and mild temperatures. Summer is hot but warm water makes it great for swimming and snorkeling.
Are there family-friendly water tours in Naples?▼
Yes — kayak eco-tours, dolphin watching tours, and glass-bottom boat tours are all popular family activities in Naples. Most operators accommodate children 6+.
