❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What activities can you do in West Florida?▼
West Florida (Tampa, Clearwater, Naples, Destin) offers jet ski rentals, boat tours, helicopter flights, kayaking, parasailing, slingshot rentals, and water sports on the Gulf Coast.
How much do West Florida water activities cost?▼
Jet ski rentals from $89. Kayaking from $25. Parasailing from $75. Boat charters from $200. Helicopter tours from $150. Premium yacht charters from $600+.
What is the best city in West Florida for water sports?▼
Clearwater Beach and Destin consistently rank as top water sports destinations. Tampa Bay offers diverse activities from kayaking to private yacht charters.
Do I need a license for West Florida water sports?▼
No license for guided tours. A Florida Boater Safety ID required if self-operating a vessel and born after January 1, 1988.
Can I book West Florida activities same day?▼
Yes. Most operators list real-time availability via FareHarbor with instant confirmation.
