💰 Miami Boat Rental Prices: What to Expect

Prices for a Miami boat rental vary widely depending on boat size, whether a captain is included, and the duration. Here's what to expect:

  • Budget (under $400): Smaller boats for 2–8 people. Great for couples, small families, or a half-day on the water. Operators like Bruschi Small Boat and Altamar Trade offer quality rentals in this range.
  • Mid-range ($400–$900): The sweet spot. Private boats for 8–13 passengers with captain included. Most birthday party, bachelorette, and corporate group bookings fall here.
  • Luxury ($900–$1,500): Premium yachts with upscale amenities. Aura Yacht and 100 Miami Luxury deliver top-tier experiences in this bracket.
  • Mega Yacht ($1,500+): For events, large parties, or VIP experiences. The 100 Miami Mega Yacht accommodates up to 20 guests at $2,000.
💡 Pro tip: Booking on a weekday saves you 15–25% compared to weekend rates. Most operators have availability Monday–Thursday with the same boats and captains as weekends.

📋 What to Know Before Renting a Boat in Miami

Do you need a license?

For most Miami boat rentals, no boating license is required. The operator provides a licensed captain and a pre-departure safety briefing. If you want a bareboat rental (you captain the boat yourself), you'll need a valid Florida boating safety certificate or equivalent.

What's included?

All Miami boat rentals include life jackets, Coast Guard-required safety equipment, and a briefing. Most private charters also include a captain, fuel, a cooler with ice, and a Bluetooth sound system. Always confirm what's included at booking.

Can I bring food and drinks?

Yes — almost all operators allow you to bring your own food and beverages (BYOB). A cooler is typically provided. Alcohol is permitted for passengers 21+ on private charters. Check the operator's specific policy when booking.

How far in advance should I book?

For weekend bookings, reserve at least 3–7 days in advance. Peak season (November–April) and holidays book out weeks ahead. Weekday bookings usually have same-day or next-day availability.
