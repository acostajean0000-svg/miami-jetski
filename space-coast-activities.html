❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What activities can you do on the Space Coast?▼
The Space Coast offers boat tours, kayaking on the Indian River Lagoon, zip-lining at Cocoa Beach, airboat rides, fishing charters, and unique Space Shuttle area experiences.
How much do Space Coast activities cost?▼
Kayaking from $35. Boat tours from $75. Zip-lining from $49. Airboat rides from $39. Fishing charters from $200.
What is the best activity on the Space Coast?▼
Kayaking on the Indian River Lagoon for bioluminescence (summer nights) is most unique. Boat tours for wildlife. Zip-lining for thrills.
Is the Space Coast good for water sports?▼
Yes — the Indian River Lagoon is one of the most biodiverse estuaries in North America. Perfect for kayaking, paddleboarding, and boat tours.

Yes. Most operators offer same-day availability via FareHarbor with instant confirmation.
