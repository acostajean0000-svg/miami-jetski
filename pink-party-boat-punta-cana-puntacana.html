FAQ – Pink Party Boat Punta Cana

What types of tours does Pink Party Boat offer? +
Party boat tours with open bar, fishing excursions, sunset cruises, and private group charters.
Is the open bar included? +
Yes, the open bar is included in party boat tours. Fishing tours have a separate menu.
How many people can the boat hold? +
Up to 30 passengers for group tours. Private charters available for smaller groups.
What time do tours depart? +
Morning fishing 6am. Party tours 10am and 3pm. Sunset cruise 5pm.
Can I book a private charter? +
Yes, private charters available for events, birthdays, and bachelorette parties.
