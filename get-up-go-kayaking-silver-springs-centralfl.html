About this Experience

Get Up and Go Kayaking at Silver Springs State Park offers stunning clear kayak tours through one of Florida's oldest natural attractions. Float over ancient springs and see fish, turtles, and alligators through your transparent kayak hull. The crystal-clear spring water provides visibility up to 50 feet down.
