❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a motorcycle license to drive a Slingshot in Miami?

No. Florida classifies the Polaris Slingshot as a motorcycle but only requires a standard driver's license (Class E) to operate it. No motorcycle endorsement needed.

Can a passenger ride in a Slingshot?

Yes. The Slingshot has a passenger seat and can accommodate two people comfortably.

What's the minimum age to rent a Slingshot in Miami?

Most operators require renters to be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license. Some may accept drivers 18+ with a higher deposit.
