FAQ – Caribbean Lake Park

What water sports are available? +
Flyboard, surf lessons, kayaking, stand-up paddleboard, and boat rides.
Do I need experience for flyboard? +
No experience needed. Instructors guide you step-by-step from the water.
What is the minimum age? +
10+ for most activities, 16+ for flyboard. Children must be accompanied by an adult.
Are life jackets provided? +
Yes, all safety equipment is included in the price.
Can I book just one activity? +
Yes, individual activities bookable or bundle packages available.
