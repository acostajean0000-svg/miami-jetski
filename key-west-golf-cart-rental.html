Key West offers jet ski tours, sailing, private boat charters, snorkeling, scuba diving, helicopter flights, golf cart rentals, fishing charters, and food/culture tours. All can be booked instantly via FareHarbor.

Prices start at $35 for lagoon tours and sunset sails. Golf cart rentals from $89/day. Jet ski tours from $120. Private boat charters from $450. Full-day fishing from $700. Private sailing from $645.
What is the best water activity in Key West?▼
Six Fins Key West is top-rated for jet ski thrills (4.5★, 100 reviews). Best Sandbar Tours is top-rated for groups (4.9★, 94 reviews). Sunset Sail Key West is best for romance (4.8★, 84 reviews). Fly Key West helicopter tours offer the most unique perspective (4.9★).
Do I need a license for Key West water activities?▼
No license needed for guided jet ski tours or passenger boat charters. A Florida Boater Safety ID is required if you self-operate a vessel and were born after January 1, 1988. Most operators provide fully-guided experiences.
