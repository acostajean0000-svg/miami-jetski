FAQ

What is the Saona Island trip like? +
A full day private boat charter departing Bayahíbe to Saona Island. Includes snorkeling stops, natural pools, beach time, and lunch.
How long is the trip? +
Approximately 7–8 hours including travel time, snorkeling, and beach lunch.
What is included in the price? +
Boat charter, snorkeling gear, life jackets, beach lunch, and water.
How many people fit on the boat? +
Private charters accommodate up to 12 passengers. Ideal for families and small groups.
When is the best time to visit Saona Island? +
Year-round. December–April offers the calmest seas and best visibility.
