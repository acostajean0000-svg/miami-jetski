📍 Best ATV Spots Near Miami

Wynwood

Wynwood Buggies offers a unique urban ATV experience through Miami's famous art district. You'll cruise past colorful murals and street art installations in an open buggy — perfect for Instagram and totally unlike any off-road experience you've had before.

Everglades Area

The closest true off-road terrain to Miami is the Everglades corridor west of the city. Several operators run guided trail rides through natural Florida landscapes — pine flatwoods, cypress swamps, and sawgrass prairies. Great for adventure seekers wanting real off-road action.

Miami Beach Adventure Parks

A handful of operators have set up dedicated ATV parks in the greater Miami area where you can ride on prepared circuits regardless of your experience level. Ideal for groups and families.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a license to rent an ATV in Miami?

Most operators require a valid driver's license. No special off-road certification is needed, but you'll receive a safety orientation before riding.

Can kids ride ATVs in Miami?

Many operators offer junior ATVs or passenger seats for kids under 16. Minimum age requirements vary by operator — typically 10–12 years old for solo riding with parental supervision.

What's the difference between an ATV and a buggy/UTV?

ATVs are single-rider four-wheelers. UTVs (utility terrain vehicles) or buggies are side-by-side vehicles that seat 2–4 people with a roll cage, making them more stable and better for groups or beginners.
